Broadband Speed

Broadband speed can vary massively depending on location, availability and signal strength. Typically, broadband speed is faster in urban areas and major cities. In rural locations and small towns, it tends to be significantly lower. However, that is rapidly changing as fibre optic broadband is rolled out to all areas in Ireland. The process has already begun and is due to be fully completed by 2020. Broadband speed can range from 16 to 1000Mbps.
