Voice Telecoms

Pure Telecom offers the highest standard of voice telecoms throughout Ireland and to the world beyond. We also offer a great many advantages over other providers:

  • Switch and Save

    By analysing your existing rates, services and equipment, Pure Telecom can streamline your overheads, giving you the optimum solution at minimum cost.

  • Per-second billing

    With no minimum charges and no set-up charges.

  • Seamless transfer

    From your previous provider – Pure Telecom simply connect your desk and your business to the world outside using your existing infrastructure.

  • Pure Connection

    Pure Telecom can provide businesses with a range of Irish and international inbound service numbers – 1800, 1850, 1890 etc. – giving your local and international customers direct access to your Irish offices. We also provide an online portal for customer controlled management of these services.

  • IP Trunking/VOIP

    It is a cost efficient way of handling your calls. We design, tailor and shape this solution for your business.

  • Conferencing

    Pure Telecom’s network offers secure ‘multiple-dial-in’ audio and visual conferencing with dependable quality which is an essential part of today’s business communication.

  • Site Planning & Project Management

    Moving office? Having a refit? We can project manage the entire installation of your telecoms, giving you the services you need at the price you want.

  • Absolute Support

    From basic advice and support to full disaster recovery and contingency plans, Pure Telecom are with you all the way.

  • Face to Face

    Account Managers meet with all our customers to develop relationship and understanding of your business telecoms requirements.
