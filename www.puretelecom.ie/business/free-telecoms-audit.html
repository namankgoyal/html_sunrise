Free Telecoms Audit

Do you know whether or not you are getting value for your business Telecoms? When did you last evaluate your equipment, services and contracts?

How many of your systems are in place simply because it seemed like too much effort to move?

Pure Telecom will undertake a free, root-and-branch audit of all your telecom systems and services. If we like what we see, we’ll say “congratulations”, if we see room for improvement – which we usually do – we’ll demonstrate where savings can be made and services improved.

The Telecoms Audit and Line Rationalisation process takes up to four weeks, including observation of usage and the shutting down of excess lines. We will meet your IT and accounts personnel and discuss your systems and lines versus your needs and the costs involved. Our customers experience significant reductions in costs and increases in efficiences following an audit from Pure Telecom. And, given the speed of change within the telecoms industry, we recommend a regular, quarterly monitoring of usage and services to ensure you are getting the maximum benefit from technology on offer – without retaining contracts you no longer require.
