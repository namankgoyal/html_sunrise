Is Fibre Broadband in My Area?

In order to check if fibre broadband is available in your area, use our Availability Checker Today. If there is a fibre network in your area, the speeds you can achieve will depend on the broadband connection. Fibre broadband hasn't reached every home in Ireland yet. However, because of the national broadband plan the number of premises passed with fibre optic cables is increasing rapidly. Unlike traditional copper wire based standard broadband, companies like Siro and NBI are aggressively expanding fibre first networks.

How Fast is Fibre Broadband?

Internet users with FTTH (fibre to the home) can reach the fastest broadband speeds of up to 1,000Mbps, while speeds for FTTC (fibre to the cabinet) will vary depending on the customer's proximity to the nearest cabinet. With zero buffering when streaming you can enjoy ultra HD clarity across Smart TV's, laptops, tablets and smartphones.

What is Better: Fibre or Broadband?

Our Fast Fibre Broadband is 10 times faster than regular broadband. That means film buffs can download a movie in less than 10 seconds, while snap-happy customers can download 1,000 of their favourite photographs in as little as five seconds.

Is Fibre Broadband Expensive?

Fibre Broadband is not expensive. Prices starting from as little as €35* per month with speeds of up to 1,000Mbps. Once you know the speeds you can reach in your area, Pure Telecom one of the best broadband providers can offer the best fibre packages to suit your needs.

Can I Get Fibre Broadband Without Phone Line?

Yes, you can get fibre broadband without a phone line. Some network providers have their own networks which do not require a phone line for connection. They can bring all fibre services directly to your home and also provide TV services via this connection. Satellite broadband services provide connection without a phone line.
