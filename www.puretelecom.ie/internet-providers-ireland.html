Work Or Play - It’s Fast!

With so many options and providers to choose from, finding the right deal isn’t always easy. Pure Telecom offers a range of options that always put our customers’ needs first. But where do you start? Check out some of the key considerations you should weigh up before deciding on your provider.

  • Price. Price is a determining factor for many customers when choosing their internet package and luckily, great broadband doesn’t have to put you out of pocket. Pure Telecom allows you to choose exactly what you want from your provider. We can offer broadband without line rental – meaning no unnecessary landline charges – which allows us to offer super-low costs for everyone.
  • Performance. In addition to great price, you also want to know your broadband connection is the best available – in terms of both reliability and speed. Pure Telecom will provide you with the fastest available speeds in your area. Our unlimited broadband also makes us the ideal provider for homes with heavy usage, like regular online gaming and video streaming.
  • Range of options. At Pure Telecom, we recognise that everyone’s needs are different, so we have a wide range of options to suit your exact needs. We offer broadband only and broadband with phone deals, lightning fast fibre broadband with speeds ranging from 150Mbps to 1,000Mbps, as well as our Next Generation Access broadband for those not yet in fibre enabled locations.
  • Support. : If you encounter a problem with your connection, you need to know that it will be resolved quickly. Our customer care team is based in Ireland and is always on-hand to help with any issues relating to your service. We have some of the best response times in the business – whether it’s over the phone or via our social media channels – and we’re known for going the extra mile for our customers.
  • Experience. Most of all, customers want a broadband provider they can trust. Pure Telecom has been offering incredible deals in Ireland since 2002. Excellent customer service is deeply embedded in our company and we are proud to offer some of the lowest cost and best performing internet options in Ireland.
