We're hiring! Inbound Sales Representatives at Pure Telecom

Due to the development and expansion of our various marketing channels, Pure Telecom is recruiting for a number of inbound sales representatives' roles to join our team. We are looking for ambitious candidates who have a proven record in closing hot prospects. Candidates must possess excellent interpersonal skills and also demonstrate a very high level of both written and verbal skills. This is a great opportunity for personal and professional development for the right candidates who have an interest in sales, marketing, and online/social media activity. The right candidate will join to become a valued member of Pure Telecoms enthusiastic and skilled team. Find out more about Pure Telecom here.

Job Responsibilities

  • To manage and close assigned inbound sales inquiries through the various channels of email, telephone, social media, and website, etc.
  • Consistently close new business subject to a monthly target
  • Achieve minimum sales targets by ensuring that all sales leads are identified, confirmed with the customer and then converted in a timely manner

Minimum Qualifications

  • 1-2 years of proven Sales/Customer service experience with a record of consistently exceeding targets
  • Previous experience as a Sales Professional in a fast-paced target driven environment as an Account Executive or sales representative
  • Experience selling in a fast-paced sales environment
  • Experience with short sales cycles and closing consumer accounts
  • Background in telecommunication service sales h2ly preferred
  • h2 ability to foster and maintain meaningful business relationships
  • Confident public speaker
  • Proficiency using Excel, MS Word, Outlook, and the Internet
  • Bachelors degree preferred
  • Telesales experience within a contact centre environment
  • Excellent written and verbal English skills

Salary

Basic 25,000 - OTE 35,000+

Citywest, Dublin
